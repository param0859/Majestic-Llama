About Llama

LLAMA FACTS

  • Llamas can carry 50 pounds for 20 miles in one day
  • Llamas have three stomachs!
  • Llamas do not have specific time of mating. Male usually chases female for up to 10 minutes until she is finally ready to mate
  • Lifespan of llamas is between 20 and 30 years

Did You Know?

  • Llama's body is covered with wool which can be black, gray, white or brown, with variety of patterns
  • Breeding, packing, wool production, companion animals and sheep guarding head the list of common llama uses
  • Llamas are easy to transport and require no specialized equipment
  • Llamas require less water than most domestic animals, but should have an unlimited, fresh, clean supply at all times

Designed By

I am currently a Junior pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Software Engineering. All of my knowledge in front end web development was gained through various websites such as Codecademy, Free Code Camp, Coursera, Udacity and others alike. My interest to learn web development started off at a young age, when I used to use software such as Adobe Dreamweaver and create simple websites to pass my time. After completing my first Introduction to Programming class, I was inspired to take on a new challenge and learn web development languages such as HTML/CSS and JavaScript. I am looking forward to becoming an intern in the web development field because it would help me to apply my skills.

This website follows modern HTML5 rules and CSS 3 styling techniques. Moreover, I wanted to use the Bootstrap framework to make my website responsive. I created a single page website that includes multiple sections to show information about myself and more importantly the Majestic Llama!
